Welcome to my academic portfolio! 

I am a Lecturer in the Information Systems Study Program at Institut Teknologi Del (IT Del), where I have been dedicated to education and research for over nine years. I also serve as the Coordinator of the Bebras Bureau at IT Del and as a Researcher at the IT Del Artificial Intelligence Research Center.

My technical expertise bridges foundational data architecture and advanced artificial intelligence. I specialize in database management, business analytics, and the development of AI-driven systems utilizing Large Language Models (LLMs), Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and AI Agents. I also hold professional certificates in Google Data Analytics and Tableau Business Intelligence Analysis.

### Research & Innovation
A core passion of mine is leveraging modern technology for cultural preservation. I am actively involved in the digitization of regional cultural assets in the Toba region. Most recently, this includes research on the automatic coloring of Ulos motifs using LLMs and Non-Dominated Sorting Differential Evolution Algorithms. 

I am also deeply engaged in shaping the future of informatics education, including drafting the market analysis and proposals for a planned Master’s Program in Artificial Intelligence at IT Del.

### Teaching & Mentoring
As a professional educator, I prioritize academic integrity, digital literacy, and the ethical use of AI. I regularly mentor students through their Information Systems Projects (PSI) and industry internships, advocating for pedagogical strategies that help the next generation of technologists overcome digital dependencies and build strong problem-solving habits.

* **Research Acceptance:** Paper accepted in the *International Journal of Advances in Intelligent Informatics* regarding the automatic coloring of Ulos motifs (February 2026).
