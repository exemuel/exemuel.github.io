This project features an interactive data visualization story hosted on Tableau Public, designed to analyze the performance and operational metrics of a railway travel network.

Project Overview

Analyzing transportation data is essential for optimizing routes, understanding passenger behavior, and improving overall business performance. This Tableau Story walks through various facets of a railway travel company's operations, transforming raw operational data into actionable business intelligence.

The primary goal of this visualization is to provide a guided, interactive exploration of ridership trends, revenue streams, and operational efficiencies across different routes and time periods.

Key Features

  • Performance Metrics: Detailed visualizations tracking key business indicators such as revenue, ticket sales, and passenger volume.
  • Trend Analysis: Time-series data exploring peak travel times, delays, and seasonal variations in ridership.
  • Interactive Storytelling: A guided analytical narrative utilizing Tableau's "Story" feature to walk viewers through the data insights step-by-step.

Tools & Technologies Used

  • Tableau: Utilized for data blending, aggregation, and crafting the interactive data story.
  • Business Analytics: Applying core data analysis principles to extract meaningful insights from complex transportation datasets.
